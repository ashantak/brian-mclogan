Kajabi behavior reveals who needs one more reason to buy.

Timing intelligence

Math education has predictable demand windows every year. Back to school in August. Midterms in October and February. Finals in December and May. The system identifies which of your 13,456 existing videos match each window and schedules a community post and Shorts re-post during that period — without a new video. High-value views from content that already exists, timed to when students most need it.
